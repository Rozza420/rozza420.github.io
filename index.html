EREBOS

Nick Dunnmore is given a sisnister but brilliant computer game
called Erebos. This game is highly addictive and asks its players
to carry out actions in the real world in order to keep playing
online - actions which become more and more manipulative.
As Nick loses friends and all sense of right and wrong in the
realworld, he gains power and advances further towards his
online goal: to beccome one of the Inner Circle of Erebos

But what is virtual and what is reality? how far will Nick
go to achieve his goal? And what does Erebos really want?
